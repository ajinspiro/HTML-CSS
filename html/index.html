Muhammad Kutty Panaparambil Ismail, (IPA: [muhɐmːɐd̪ kuʈːi pɐnɐpːɐrɐmbil ismɐjl]), (born 7 September 1951),[2] better known by his stage name Mammootty, (IPA: [mɐmːuːʈːi]), is an Indian actor and film producer who works predominantly in Malayalam cinema. In a career spanning five decades, he has acted in over 400 films, mainly in Malayalam, with several in Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Hindi and English.[6][7][8] His acting and contributions to Malayalam cinema have been praised by his contemporaries in the Indian film industry.[9][10][11]
